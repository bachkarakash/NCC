About Us


National Computing Contest(Clash) is held in PICT campus on the 14th of Feb'17. It is organised by PICT-IEEE Student Branch in collaboration with Nalini Foundation. The event is being organized since past few years and it is regarded as reputed national level computing contest. In keeping with the tradition of bringing the best coders of the city under one roof.PISB is organizing Clash.

The compition comprises of several algorithmic problems of varing difficulty, wich are to be solved by participants in C/C++. So are you ready to analyse algorithms,search strinds and traverse trees to climb your way up to victory?
